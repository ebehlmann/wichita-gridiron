THANK YOU

Thank you for contributing to a Society of Professional Journalists - Kansas Pro Chapter scholarship!

Because of the support of people like you, SPJ has awarded more than $230,000 in scholarships to up-and-coming journalists across Kansas.

This year's winners will be announced at the Wichita Gridiron show. We hope to see you there.

If your donation qualifies you for a "thank you" gift, a member of the SPJ board will follow up via email in the coming days. Please look for an email from info@wichitagridiron.com.
