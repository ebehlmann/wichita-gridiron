RANDY BROWN CURTAIN CALL AFTER-PARTY

Celebrate Gridiron’s 50th Anniversary. Party with the cast and crew, including vocal director Karla Burns, at the Randy Brown Curtain Call — A Journalism Fundraiser.

Held at the Orpheum after the Friday show, enjoy music by Pop and the Boys. Tickets cost $50 and include after-show nibbles, two drink coupons (plus a cash bar) and a chance to win Riverfest buttons.

Proceeds from the party will go toward the SPJ's new Randy Brown Memorial Scholarship Fund. Randy Brown was a long-time Wichita journalist, Gridironer and advocate for transparency in government. He also taught at Wichita State University and led the Kansas Sunshine Coalition for Open Government. He died in 2014.

Celebrate 50 years of Gridiron. Help fund a new scholarship. Eat. Drink. Dance. Bid on the silent auction. Win. Have fun. Mostly have fun.

Must be 21+ to attend.

Organized by the Kansas Pro Chapter of the Society of Professional Journalists, funding journalism scholarships statewide since 1968.
