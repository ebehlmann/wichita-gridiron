Proceeds from the party fund the SPJ's Randy Brown Memorial Scholarship Fund. Randy Brown was a long-time Wichita journalist, Gridironer and advocate for transparency in government. He also taught at Wichita State University and led the Kansas Sunshine Coalition for Open Government. He died in 2014.
