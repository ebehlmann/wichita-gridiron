CURTAIN CALL AFTER-PARTY

Celebrate Gridiron’s 50th Anniversary. Party with the cast and crew, including vocal director Karla Burns, at Curtain Call — A Journalism Fundraiser.

Held at the Orpheum after the Friday show, enjoy music by Pop and the Boys. Tickets cost $50 and include after-show nibbles, two drink coupons (plus a cash bar) and a chance to win Riverfest buttons.

Celebrate 50 years of Gridiron. Eat. Drink. Dance. Bid on the silent auction. Win. Have fun. Mostly have fun.

Must be 21+ to attend.

Organized by the Kansas Pro Chapter of the Society of Professional Journalists, funding journalism scholarships statewide since 1968.
