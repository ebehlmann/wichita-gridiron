RANDY BROWN CURTAIN CALL AFTER-PARTY

Held at the Orpheum after the 50th anniversary Friday show on April 6, 2018, the first Randy Brown Curtain Call featured mingling with cast and crew, plus music by Pop and the Boys.

Proceeds from the party will go toward the SPJ's new Randy Brown Memorial Scholarship Fund. Randy Brown was a long-time Wichita journalist, Gridironer and advocate for transparency in government. He also taught at Wichita State University and led the Kansas Sunshine Coalition for Open Government. He died in 2014.

Thank you to all who attended!

The Randy Brown Curtain Call Pary will return in 2019. Stay tuned for details.

Organized by the Kansas Pro Chapter of the Society of Professional Journalists, funding journalism scholarships statewide since 1968.
